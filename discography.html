Paul Smoker Discography

Paul Smoker's recording career began in the era of the LP, continued through the CD period, and ended during the rise of streaming. His performances were documented on various US and international labels as well as artist-released editions. Consequently, some recordings are available on some purchase/streaming platforms but not others, and some are currently out of print and unavailable to stream. A prime source for hardcopy CDs may be found through CornerStoreJazz (use their contact page to inquire about CDs not yet listed on their site) as well as downloading directly from the Paul Smoker Bandcamp page. The Bop Shop in Rochester, NY, often has Paul Smoker CDs or LPs in stock, and many titles (mainly used) can be bought or sold through Discogs. We will strive to update this page as more recordings become available.

The discography begins with Paul's albums as a leader, continues with several projects as a co-leader, and concludes with his recordings as a sideperson. Use the "Index" (at left, or at top on mobile devices) to navigate quickly to recordings and projects of particular interest.
